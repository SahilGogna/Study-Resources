## Key Metrics to Track

- **Skills Progress:** Courses completed, projects built

---

## Pro Tips

1. **Consistency > Intensity:** 4 focused hours daily beats 10 random hours
2. **Quality > Quantity:** 5 tailored applications beat 50 generic ones
3. **Build in Public:** Share your learning journey on LinkedIn
4. **Network Genuinely:** Help others, don't just ask for referrals
5. **Document Everything:** Every project is portfolio material
6. **Mental Health Matters:** Take breaks, job search is a marathon

---

**Remember:** December and early January see slower hiring. Use this time strategically to upskill and position yourself for the hiring surge in late January/February.
